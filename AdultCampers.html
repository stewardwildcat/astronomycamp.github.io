Former Adult Campers
Randy Henzler: Started his own management firm and was a leader in developing the current SkyCenter educational facilities atop Mt. Lemmon. He's also become active in Seattle's Astronomy Club and volunteers in the public schools, hosting astronomy sessions for students.

Janet Howard: Has continued to pursue her astronomy education through course work and traveling across the globe. She has become a Solar System Ambassador for NASA. She has also been a data analyst for the Stardust mission.

Marsha Wolf: Received her PhD in astronomy at the University of Texas (Austin) in 2005 and is now a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Wisconsin - Madison. She helped commission the 9m HET telescope and is involved in the SOAR telescope.

John Chumack: Is a prominent astrophotographer whose celestial images of the Sun, planets, meteors, stars, and galaxies are sought-after by the national media. He shares a wonderfully illustrated summary of his Advanced Adult Camp (1996) in this journal.

If you know of any other news about former Campers and Counselors, please email dmccarthy@as.arizona.edu.
